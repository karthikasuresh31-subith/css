SAMSUNG

The Samsung Galaxy S25 series introduces several special features, including advanced AI capabilities like Live Translate and Circle to Search, enhanced cameras with a new ProVisual Engine and 8K video recording, the ultra-durable Gorilla Armor 2 display on the Ultra model, and a refreshed One UI 7 with improved home screen customization for icon sizing and app labels. The S25 Ultra also boasts a robust titanium frame and superior durability, while the S25 Edge offers an exceptionally thin profile.
